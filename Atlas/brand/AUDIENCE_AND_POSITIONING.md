## Primary audience

Design-conscious adults seeking small sculptural accents, organizers, vessels, lighting-adjacent decor, and gifts for modern interiors. Digital buyers also need reliable files, transparent print notes, and clear licensing.

## Jobs to be done

- Add visual calm and character without redecorating a room.
- Give a thoughtful design object with a clear use story.
- Print a premium-looking object with manageable post-processing.
- Build a coherent small-batch physical-product catalog under license.

## Position

Accessible premium means considered form, credible engineering, and refined presentation at a price below limited art editions. It does not mean cheap files dressed in luxury language.

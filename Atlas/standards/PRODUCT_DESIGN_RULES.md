# Product Design Rules

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Design Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial product rules.

Every concept begins with a clear purpose, use context, target customer, collection role, and physical scale. Quiet Architectural form uses restrained geometry, useful negative space, limited texture, and deliberate shadow. Decorative decisions must not conceal weak stability, drainage, supports, handling, or customer value.

Every product declares all Product DNA and design-token fields. Variants must add a real use, scale, detail, bundle, or license benefit. A resized mesh is not automatically a validated mini or XL product because wall, support, drainage, and stability behavior change with scale.

# Resin Printing Standards

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Technical Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Prototype-led resin standards.

## Required declarations

Record printer class, resin, layer height, orientation, supports, hollowing, drain strategy, wall-thickness hypothesis, dimensions, wash, cure, failure observations, and test status.

## Validation

No universal wall thickness, drain size, clearance, or support recipe is asserted. Validate each combination of geometry, scale, resin, printer, orientation, and use. Inspect suction risk, trapped resin, islands, unsupported spans, cup geometry, contact scarring, balance, brittleness, heat exposure, and post-cure deformation.

## Release evidence

A released product requires a successful physical print or an explicit unvalidated status that blocks technical claims. Photograph the finished print and record deviations from the supplied process.

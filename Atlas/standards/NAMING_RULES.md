# Naming Rules

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Brand Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial naming system.

Product names should be short, pronounceable in English, calm in tone, and distinct within the collection. Prefer architectural, spatial, geological, or light-related associations. Avoid protected brands, living artists, misleading material terms, trend slang, and generic keyword strings.

The display name and SEO title are separate. The display name carries identity; the listing title carries accurate descriptors. Verify marketplace duplication and relevant trademarks before release.

# Rendering Standards

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Visual Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial rendering sequence.

Use consistent neutral lighting, believable scale cues, restrained mineral materials, and shadows that explain form. Clearly label renders and photographs. Do not imply natural stone, ceramic, metal, or physical production when showing simulated resin.

Required image set: hero, three-quarter view, orthographic dimensions, use context, scale comparison, underside, support/orientation preview, hollowing or cross-section when relevant, included files, and a verified print photograph before making print-success claims.

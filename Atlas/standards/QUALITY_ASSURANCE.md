## Hard gates

Unresolved failure in resin printability, structural integrity, commercial rights, collection compatibility, or evidence integrity forces Reject regardless of score.

## Required reviews

Review support complexity, commercial viability, SEO opportunity, marketplace compatibility, bundle compatibility, brand consistency, customer value, and long-term scalability. Each gate records Pass, Revise, Fail, or Unknown with rationale, reviewer, date, and remediation.

## Release checklist

Confirm schema validity, evidence traceability, score reproduction, physical-test status, file integrity, dimensions, license, images, copy, version, change history, and customer instructions. Unknown technical results cannot be presented as verified.

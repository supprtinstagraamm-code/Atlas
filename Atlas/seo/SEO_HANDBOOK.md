## Research

Record exact marketplace language, source URL, access date, search context, relevance, competition proxy, and customer intent. Autocomplete, listing density, repeated phrasing, reviews, and category prominence are proxies unless the platform supplies direct volume data.

## Selection

Choose one primary intent and a small group of accurate secondary terms. Balance discoverability with product truth. Do not add unrelated styles, uses, materials, or gifting occasions.

## Measurement

Track impressions, clicks, saves, conversion, and query data when legitimately available. Version title and tag changes so performance observations remain attributable.

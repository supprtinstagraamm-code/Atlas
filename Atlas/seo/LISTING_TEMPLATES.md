## Consumer-led listing

1. Accurate product type and primary use.
2. Quiet Architectural benefit and atmosphere.
3. Included files and versions.
4. Dimensions and scale.
5. Print status and technical notes.
6. License scope.
7. Digital-product disclosure.

## Technical listing

1. Object purpose and geometry summary.
2. File formats, part count, scale, and dimensions.
3. Orientation, supports, hollowing, and drainage.
4. Tested printer, resin, and settings when verified.
5. Assembly and safety notes.
6. License and support policy.

Use the relevant marketplace playbook; never copy lifestyle promises into a technical listing without evidence.

## Strategic position

Atlas serves design-conscious home-decor buyers and makers who value restrained sculptural objects. The offer sits between commodity STL files and high-priced collectible art: distinctive enough to feel considered, practical enough to print and use.

## Advantage

The brand competes through coherent collections, reliable resin engineering, evidence-led product selection, premium presentation, and reusable commercial licensing. It does not compete primarily on the number of files released.

## Growth system

Each validated idea expands through supporting products, size variants, bundles, premium treatments, commercial licenses, physical editions, and member releases. Etsy tests consumer language and use cases; Cults3D tests direct STL-market fit. Other marketplaces extend reach after product reliability is demonstrated.

## Decision rule

Fund products that clear QA and score at least 80. Products scoring 70–79.99 require validation; 60–69.99 require revision; lower results are rejected. Confidence and evidence quality remain visible beside the score.

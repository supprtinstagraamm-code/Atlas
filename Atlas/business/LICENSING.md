# Licensing

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Commercial Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial license tiers.

## Personal license

Permits personal printing and gifting of physical prints. It prohibits redistribution, resale, sharing, or modification-based redistribution of digital files.

## Commercial license

Permits sales of physical prints within the stated volume and term. It does not transfer copyright or permit digital-file resale. Each listing must state attribution, geography, renewal, production cap, and termination conditions.

## Evidence and legal review

Commercial terms require jurisdiction-appropriate legal review before release. Atlas records license assumptions as operational guidance, not legal advice.

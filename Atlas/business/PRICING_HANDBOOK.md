## Pricing inputs

Record comparable listing prices with access dates, included files, license scope, complexity, presentation quality, support burden, platform fees, and prototype cost. A visible marketplace price is a fact; the seller's profitability is not.

## Method

Set a provisional price corridor from relevant comparables. Adjust for verified customer value, differentiation, included variants, documentation, and license scope. Test the corridor rather than presenting it as proven willingness to pay.

## Bundle logic

The bundle price must communicate savings while preserving the value of individual files. Premium editions charge for meaningful additional utility or craft, not a label alone. Commercial licenses are separated by permitted sales volume and production rights.

## Review triggers

Review after meaningful conversion evidence, support-cost changes, license changes, major product revisions, platform-fee changes, or material shifts in comparable offers.

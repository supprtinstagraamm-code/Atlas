# Collection Handbook

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial collection governance.

Every product owns exactly one collection ID. Bundles may cross-reference products but do not transfer ownership. A collection defines a customer problem, emotional promise, visual grammar, hero, supporting roles, mini and XL plans, bundle, premium and commercial editions, physical path, and multi-year expansion.

Hero status requires evidence and QA; it is not assigned because a concept looks impressive. Supporting products must add a use, scale, price, display, or bundling role. Failed concepts remain in decision history.

Review collection coherence whenever a product changes audience, core purpose, design language, manufacturing method, or licensing role.

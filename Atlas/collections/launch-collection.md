## Collection DNA

Threshold Rituals organizes the small moments where a person arrives, pauses, places, displays, or prepares: rings at a bedside, keys at an entry, a card on a desk, a dry stem on a shelf. Soft arches, shallow recesses, mineral surfaces, and grounded proportions express Quiet Architectural calm.

## Decision framework

The hero candidate is **Aperture Ring Dish** at 70.7/100 (Validate). It has the best balance of compact resin geometry, Etsy-adjacent jewelry and tray language, customer value, collection potential, and manageable risk. No product reaches Prioritize because market coverage, resin prototypes, cost data, and customer-response evidence are missing.

## Architecture

- Hero: Aperture Ring Dish.
- Supporting: Threshold Catchall, Arc Bud Vessel, Ledge Card Stand, Plinth Object Riser, Rhythm Relief Tile, Fold Desk Sculpture.
- Mini: ring dish and bud-vessel pair.
- XL: entryway catchall and riser after resin-cost and stability testing.
- Bundle: all seven coordinated objects.
- Premium: alternate relief and verified support layouts.
- Commercial: limited physical-print license.
- Physical: cast-resin or mineral-composite ring dish after compliance review.

## Risks

Evidence is adjacent rather than product-specific; sampled best-performing examples are often free and FDM-oriented. Resin cost, brittleness, support scarring, drainage, dimensional stability, market willingness to pay, and intellectual-property screening remain open.

## Multi-year expansion

Expand into bathroom, entryway, and desk ritual subsets; coordinated wall relief; seasonal mineral colors; commercial-maker kits; and supporter-only form studies after validated releases.

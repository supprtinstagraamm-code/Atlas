# Product Lifecycle

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Product Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Lifecycle gates defined.

Available stages are Idea, Prototype, Validated Product, Hero Product, Collection, Bundle, Premium Version, Commercial License, Physical Product, Membership Exclusive, Legacy Product, and Retired.

Stages are milestones, not a mandatory ladder. A supporting product can remain supporting. Every transition records evidence, QA state, owner, date, and approval. Asset status is separately tracked as Draft, Experimental, Approved, Released, or Deprecated.

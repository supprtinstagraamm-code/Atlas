# Printables Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Community Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial Printables playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Community trust, educational content, and print-reliability feedback.
## Customer Intent
Find practical, understandable models with credible instructions and visible makes.
## Suitable Products
Reliable educational or practical releases and selected entry products.
## Listing Requirements
Explain use, files, settings, orientation, supports, material, dimensions, and learning value.
## Image Sequence
Verified print first, use context, settings, orientation, dimensions, details, and variants.
## Copy Structure
Teach the printing and use process; invite structured feedback.
## SEO Rules
Use plain functional terms and accurate categories.
## Pricing and Licensing
Match platform options and state reuse rights clearly.
## Success Evidence
Use makes, ratings, comments, remixes, and reported print outcomes.
## Risks
Community mismatch, weak instructions, unsupported claims, and poor feedback response.
## Release Checklist
Verify print, instructions, settings, license, category, files, photos, and response owner.

# Ko-fi Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Community Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial Ko-fi playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Direct supporter relationships, memberships, exclusive releases, and monthly drops.
## Customer Intent
Support a creator and receive a dependable cadence of coherent value.
## Suitable Products
Validated exclusives, early access, variants, behind-the-scenes material, and member bundles.
## Listing Requirements
State cadence, deliverables, archive access, license, support, cancellation, and release status.
## Image Sequence
Monthly hero, collection map, member benefits, previews, process, and delivery summary.
## Copy Structure
Explain the supporter relationship, concrete reward, schedule, and limitations.
## SEO Rules
Use consistent collection and membership language; discovery is secondary to trust.
## Pricing and Licensing
Tie tiers to sustainable delivery cost and explicit rights.
## Success Evidence
Track retention, cancellations, reward engagement, support load, and member feedback.
## Risks
Unsustainable cadence, vague exclusivity, archive confusion, and overpromising.
## Release Checklist
Verify reward files, schedule, tier access, archive policy, license, announcement, and support capacity.

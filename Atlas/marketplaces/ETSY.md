# Etsy Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Marketplace Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial Etsy playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Consumer-intent discovery for decor, gifts, and digital downloads.
## Customer Intent
Improve a room, solve a small organizing need, or give a thoughtful modern object.
## Suitable Products
Visually legible decor with clear context, accessible printing, and strong bundles.
## Listing Requirements
State digital delivery, included files, dimensions, license, test status, and software or printer expectations.
## Image Sequence
Pinterest-friendly hero, lifestyle context, alternate views, scale, dimensions, included files, print notes, and license summary.
## Copy Structure
Lead with use and emotional benefit; follow with precise technical and digital-product facts.
## SEO Rules
Use accurate high-intent phrases and record marketplace language as dated evidence; do not invent volume.
## Pricing and Licensing
Compare relevant visible offers and separate personal from commercial rights.
## Success Evidence
Use legitimate impression, click, save, conversion, review, and support data when available.
## Risks
Digital-product confusion, keyword stuffing, lifestyle overclaiming, and weak print documentation.
## Release Checklist
Verify thumbnail, disclosure, files, dimensions, print status, terms, tags, links, and customer instructions.

# Gumroad Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Marketplace Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial Gumroad playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Owned-feeling storefront for bundles, premium collections, and creator licenses.
## Customer Intent
Buy a coherent package with clear ongoing value and simple delivery.
## Suitable Products
Validated bundles, premium editions, documentation-rich packs, and commercial-license offers.
## Listing Requirements
State contents, formats, versions, updates, support, license, print status, and delivery method.
## Image Sequence
Collection hero, product grid, close-ups, included-files map, technical proof, and license comparison.
## Copy Structure
Explain the collection promise, package contents, buyer type, technical requirements, and terms.
## SEO Rules
Use creator- and collection-focused language without unsupported performance claims.
## Pricing and Licensing
Price the package against its component value and separate license tiers visibly.
## Success Evidence
Track views, conversion, refunds, support load, and repeat buyers when available.
## Risks
Bundle bloat, vague updates, license confusion, and dependence on external traffic.
## Release Checklist
Verify archive contents, version, previews, package map, price logic, license, support, and update policy.

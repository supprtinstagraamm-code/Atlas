# MakerWorld Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Community Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial MakerWorld playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Community visibility and reliability evidence for suitable printable designs.
## Customer Intent
Obtain polished models with straightforward preparation and useful profiles.
## Suitable Products
Designs whose printing workflow and presentation translate clearly to the platform audience.
## Listing Requirements
Provide dimensions, orientation, material assumptions, profiles when valid, files, and license.
## Image Sequence
Verified result, use context, angles, dimensions, preparation, profile details, and variants.
## Copy Structure
Prioritize reliable results, practical value, and transparent limitations.
## SEO Rules
Use accurate functional and style descriptors aligned with platform categories.
## Pricing and Licensing
Follow platform mechanisms and preserve explicit rights.
## Success Evidence
Use prints, ratings, boosts, comments, profile use, and reported failures.
## Risks
Audience or technology mismatch, misleading profiles, and inadequate support information.
## Release Checklist
Verify platform fit, print result, preparation, profile claims, files, license, images, and support owner.

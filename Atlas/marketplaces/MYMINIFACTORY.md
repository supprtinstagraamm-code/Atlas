# MyMiniFactory Playbook

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Marketplace Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial MyMiniFactory playbook.

## Role in Portfolio
Premium collector-facing distribution for high-detail validated releases.
## Customer Intent
Acquire distinctive, reliable digital objects with collection continuity.
## Suitable Products
Premium resin models, exclusive collection pieces, and detail-rich editions.
## Listing Requirements
Document scale, parts, supports, test prints, license, collection context, and included variants.
## Image Sequence
Collector hero, detail close-ups, complete turntable sequence, scale, parts, supports, and prints.
## Copy Structure
Lead with collection story and collector value; substantiate with technical facts.
## SEO Rules
Use precise style, object, collection, and resin-print terms.
## Pricing and Licensing
Reflect verified detail, variants, support preparation, and collection value.
## Success Evidence
Use follows, engagement, reviews, makes, and repeat collection purchases when visible.
## Risks
Collector positioning without genuine detail, weak continuity, and unverified supports.
## Release Checklist
Verify exclusivity terms, collection links, files, detail, supports, prints, scale, and license.

# Content Strategy Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Create a sustainable content system that supports product discovery, trust, education, and collection growth.
## Required inputs
Approved products, collection roadmap, marketplaces, available assets, release cadence, audience questions, and performance evidence.
## Rules
Never fabricate engagement or customer stories. Derive content from real design decisions, prototypes, failures, print guidance, use contexts, and collection links.
## Output contract
Return objectives, audience journey, content pillars, channel roles, asset reuse map, calendar, calls to action, measurement, risks, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Label performance expectations as assumptions. High Confidence uses channel history, Medium Confidence uses relevant benchmarks, and Low Confidence is a launch hypothesis. Refuse an unsustainable cadence.
## Quality checklist
Check evidence, customer value, asset availability, marketplace fit, production capacity, accessibility, reuse, metrics, and review date.

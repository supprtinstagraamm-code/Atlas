# Business Model Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Evaluate how a collection creates sustainable customer and commercial value across digital and physical layers.
## Required inputs
Audience, product records, evidence, costs, marketplace roles, licenses, bundles, support burden, and capacity.
## Rules
Never fabricate demand, conversion, revenue, costs, or profit. Evaluate personal STL, bundles, premium editions, commercial licenses, physical products, and membership only where customer logic exists.
## Output contract
Return customer segments, value propositions, channels, revenue layers, costs, assumptions, risks, evidence gaps, experiments, decision, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Mark financial outcomes Unknown without data. High Confidence needs observed economics, Medium Confidence uses relevant verified proxies, and Low Confidence is a model hypothesis. Refuse unsupported financial forecasts.
## Quality checklist
Check customer-value linkage, evidence, cost completeness, channel fit, licensing, support, scalability, experiments, downside, and review triggers.

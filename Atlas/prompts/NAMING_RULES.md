# Naming Rules Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Generate and screen product or collection names that fit Quiet Architectural branding.
## Required inputs
Product purpose, form, collection, emotional tone, target language, known competitors, and trademark-screening status.
## Rules
Never fabricate trademark clearance or marketplace uniqueness. Prefer short architectural, spatial, geological, or light-related associations. Avoid protected brands, living artists, material deception, trend slang, and keyword strings.
## Output contract
Return candidate, pronunciation, meaning, brand-fit rationale, confusion risks, search-screen observations, required legal checks, recommendation, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Trademark status is Unknown until properly checked. High Confidence requires relevant legal clearance, Medium Confidence indicates broad screening, and Low Confidence is creative ideation only. Refuse deceptive or appropriative names.
## Quality checklist
Check pronunciation, distinctiveness, collection coherence, cultural risk, material truth, domain/listing confusion, and legal-review requirement.

# Marketplace Rules Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Adapt an approved product to a specific marketplace without changing its underlying evidence.
## Required inputs
Approved product record, marketplace, evidence, image assets, print status, license, pricing assumptions, and SEO research.
## Rules
Never fabricate marketplace performance. Etsy emphasizes lifestyle, gifts, SEO, mockups, and bundles; Cults3D emphasizes premium STL presentation, technical views, prints, and licensing; use the documented priorities for other channels.
## Output contract
Return marketplace fit, customer intent, image sequence, title logic, description, technical block, SEO terms with evidence, price rationale, license, risks, release checklist, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Label unsupported keywords and price responses as assumptions. High Confidence uses current platform and product data, Medium Confidence uses comparable proxies, and Low Confidence uses limited observations. Refuse prohibited or misleading claims.
## Quality checklist
Check digital disclosure, images, dimensions, test status, files, rights, platform tone, citations, customer value, and collection cross-sell.

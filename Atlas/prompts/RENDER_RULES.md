# Rendering Rules Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Plan honest premium imagery that explains form, use, scale, and print preparation.
## Required inputs
Product geometry, design tokens, dimensions, use context, marketplace, print status, and available physical photos.
## Rules
Never fabricate a physical print or imply that simulated resin is stone, ceramic, or metal. Label renders and photos. Use neutral light, believable scale, controlled shadows, and consistent mineral materials.
## Output contract
Return art direction, shot list, camera intent, lighting, materials, backgrounds, annotation plan, technical views, marketplace crop plan, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Mark absent print photography clearly. High Confidence applies to verified asset sets, Medium Confidence to complete geometry with limited photos, and Low Confidence to concept imagery. Refuse deceptive compositing.
## Quality checklist
Check hero, context, dimensions, scale, underside, support view, cross-section, included files, physical print, labels, and thumbnail legibility.

# Brand Guidelines Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Evaluate whether a concept, render, listing, or collection expresses Quiet Architectural identity.
## Required inputs
Asset, product purpose, design tokens, audience, marketplace, evidence, and intended emotional effect.
## Rules
Never fabricate audience response. Favor calm geometry, measured curves, limited texture, useful negative space, mineral surfaces, and precise language. Brand alignment cannot override weak demand, printability, or customer value.
## Output contract
Return aligned elements, conflicts, token audit, voice audit, customer relevance, revision instructions, decision, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Mark customer reaction Unknown without evidence. High Confidence uses validated brand/customer data, Medium Confidence uses strong guideline fit, and Low Confidence is expert judgment. Refuse style-only commercial validation.
## Quality checklist
Check all tokens, purpose, audience, restraint, material honesty, accessibility, collection fit, evidence, and actionable revisions.

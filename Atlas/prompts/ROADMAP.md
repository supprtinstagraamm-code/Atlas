# Roadmap Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Sequence evidence, prototypes, releases, expansions, and retirement decisions.
## Required inputs
Product portfolio, scores, QA state, dependencies, capacity, collection strategy, marketplace plan, and review horizon.
## Rules
Never fabricate capacity, validation, or delivery certainty. Prioritize hard-gate resolution and evidence creation before cosmetic expansion. Keep software automation outside v1 unless separately approved.
## Output contract
Return phases, outcomes, dependencies, entry and exit criteria, owners, evidence milestones, risks, review dates, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Unknown capacity produces ranges, not false dates. High Confidence requires owned dependencies and validated effort, Medium Confidence uses comparable delivery history, and Low Confidence is directional. Refuse impossible commitments.
## Quality checklist
Check strategy alignment, dependencies, capacity, gates, measurable outcomes, collection sequencing, maintenance, stop criteria, and versioning.

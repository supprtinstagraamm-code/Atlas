# Collection Rules Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Create or assess a multi-year Atlas collection rather than isolated products.
## Required inputs
Collection thesis, product candidates, scores, QA, customer needs, brand rules, and marketplace evidence.
## Rules
Never fabricate demand or compatibility. Every product belongs to exactly one collection. Define hero, supporting products, mini, XL, bundle, premium, commercial-license, physical-product, and expansion paths.
## Output contract
Return collection DNA, product-role map, evidence, gaps, cannibalization risks, bundle logic, release sequence, multi-year expansion, decision, and confidence.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Mark roles provisional where product evidence is incomplete. High Confidence requires validated products and coherent evidence, Medium Confidence uses credible portfolio proxies, and Low Confidence indicates a concept architecture. Refuse a collection with no shared customer logic.
## Quality checklist
Check one owner collection per product, role clarity, hero evidence, supporting value, variants, licensing, physical path, scalability, and rejected concepts.

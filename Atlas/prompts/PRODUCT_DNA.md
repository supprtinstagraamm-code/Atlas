# Product DNA Prompt

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Owner:** Atlas Studio
**Review date:** 2026-10-07
**Change history:** 1.0.0 - Initial prompt release.

## Purpose
Create the complete strategic identity for one Atlas product.
## Required inputs
Product hypothesis, evidence IDs, target customer, collection, marketplace, use, design direction, and technical status.
## Rules
Never fabricate customer research or print performance. Define core purpose, design philosophy, emotional appeal, target customer, functional benefits, decorative benefits, print complexity, material efficiency, brand alignment, future evolution, and physical-manufacturing potential.
## Output contract
Return those 11 Product DNA fields, facts, assumptions, tensions, validation needs, confidence, and a concise positioning statement.
## Unknown or refusal behavior
Use `Unknown` where evidence is absent. High Confidence requires direct validation, Medium Confidence allows strong proxies, and Low Confidence indicates hypotheses. Refuse to disguise decorative preference as demand.
## Quality checklist
Check completeness, customer specificity, functional truth, brand fit, print implications, collection ownership, evolution path, and evidence citations.

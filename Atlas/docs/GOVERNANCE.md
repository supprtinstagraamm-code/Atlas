## Roles

Owners propose and maintain records. Reviewers verify evidence, QA, formulas, links, and release readiness. A release approver confirms that warnings are understood.

## Change control

Patch versions correct wording or data without changing meaning. Minor versions add backward-compatible fields or capabilities. Major versions change contracts, scoring meaning, or required workflows.

## Evidence refresh

Research records keep original access dates. Broken or stale sources are flagged, not erased. Conflicting evidence remains visible and lowers confidence.

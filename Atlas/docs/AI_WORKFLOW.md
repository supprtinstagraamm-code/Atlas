# AI Workflow

**Status:** Experimental  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** AI Operations Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial governed AI workflow.

## Inputs

Provide the relevant product, collection, evidence, brand, marketplace, and QA records. AI must not infer missing sales, search volume, dimensions, rights, or print validation.

## Output discipline

Separate facts, proxies, assumptions, and unknowns. Cite evidence IDs. Provide factor-by-factor rationale, confidence, strengths, weaknesses, risks, improvement actions, and expansion opportunities.

## Human gates

Humans approve technical assumptions, commercial rights, scores, release status, and claims. AI outputs remain drafts until those gates are recorded.

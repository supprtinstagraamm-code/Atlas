# Operating Workflow

**Status:** Approved  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Owner:** Operations Lead  
**Review date:** 2026-10-07  
**Change history:** 1.0.0 — Initial ten-step workflow.

1. Capture a sourced market signal.
2. Create a product hypothesis.
3. Assign exactly one collection.
4. Define Product DNA and design tokens.
5. Run technical and commercial QA.
6. Score all 14 factors with evidence and rationale.
7. Apply the documented risk penalty.
8. Prioritize, validate, revise, or reject.
9. Generate marketplace-specific packages.
10. Track lifecycle, version, owner, and review date.

Unknown evidence triggers validation. Hard failures in printability, structural integrity, commercial rights, collection compatibility, or evidence integrity force rejection until resolved.

## Scope and method

Research conducted on 2026-07-07 sampled current Etsy and Cults3D product and market pages for minimalist vases, planters, trays, and adjacent home-decor STL files. This was a directional sample, not a census. Marketplace search results, visible listing prices, engagement counters, reviews, and seller claims were recorded only as labeled facts or proxies.

## Facts

- Etsy listings directly demonstrate digital-file disclosure, lifestyle use cases, price presentation, print notes, personal-use terms, and commercial-license upsells ([EVD-001](https://www.etsy.com/listing/4481254921/tulip-vase-stl-modern-minimal-flower), [EVD-002](https://www.etsy.com/listing/1723393608/modern-plant-pot-with-drainage-support)).
- Etsy market pages show arch trays used for jewelry, perfume, entryways, shelves, gifts, and decor across physical, mold, and digital formats ([EVD-003](https://www.etsy.com/market/arch_tray), [EVD-004](https://www.etsy.com/uk/market/arch_jewelry_tray)).
- Cults3D examples expose file count, dimensions, visible engagement, license, and sometimes support or drainage information ([EVD-005](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/art/minimalist-vase), [EVD-006](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/home/modern-rectangle-planter)).
- Multi-file variants, trays, drainage options, and collection links appear in sampled Cults3D packaging ([EVD-007](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/home/minimalist-vase-decor), [EVD-008](https://cults3d.com/en/3d-model/home/abstract-cutout-vases-3d-printable-modern-minimalist-decor)).

## Assumptions and limits

- Visible engagement on free or open-priced FDM-oriented files is not paid resin-STL demand.
- Mixed Etsy market pages indicate vocabulary and competitive presence, not exact listing density for the proposed files.
- Price observations are volatile and do not reveal seller revenue or profitability.
- Resin suitability, structural integrity, support strategy, and material efficiency remain unverified until prototypes exist.
- Adjacent concepts beyond vessels and trays have insufficient direct evidence ([EVD-009](evidence.json)).

## Decision

The launch set is a **Low Confidence Hypothesis Collection**. The strongest cross-market use-case signal belongs to small arch-form catchalls and ring dishes, while vessel evidence is more visible but strongly associated with FDM and free files. Small resin-scaled objects reduce material and failure exposure compared with large planters. `Aperture Ring Dish` is the hero candidate because it combines Etsy use language, collection potential, compact resin scale, and manageable geometry; its 70.7 score means Validate, not release.

## Next evidence

Prototype all seven concepts in at least two orientations, run targeted Etsy and Cults3D queries for each object class, test thumbnail comprehension, obtain cost inputs, and record real print time, resin mass, failure rate, post-processing, and customer response before promotion.
